Partner with Us

Heremi will connect you to more consumers in Davao City. No initial fees needed, just simply advertise your services and products to online users.

Licensed Physicians

  1. Download the app in the available app stores.
  2. Your service will be posted online including necessary details, for the users to see.
  3. The primary goal is offer diagnosis through teleconsultation using the app.
  4. E-prescription is made available after diagnosis.
  5. Dynamic pricing will be implemented for consultation fees.
  6. Earnings gathered are withdrawable based on the choosen payment option.
  7. Heremi takes 6% of the total earnings.
  8. An admin console, accessible through web browser, will be provided where you can manage your appointments, transactions, patient analytics, and more.

Pharmacy Owners

  1. Download the app in the available app stores.
  2. Products from your pharmacy will be posted online including necessary details, for the users to see.
  3. The primary goal is offer online shopping for medical products using the app.
  4. E-prescription products are included in requirement of doctor's note.
  5. Dynamic pricing will be implemented for the product prices.
  6. Earnings gathered are withdrawable based on the choosen payment option.
  7. Heremi takes 6% of the total earnings.
  8. An admin console, accessible through web browser, will be provided where you can manage your inquiries, stocks, transactions, sales, and more.

Courier Services

  1. Download the app in the available app stores.
  2. Every order generated will be delivered by the courier service.
  3. Using the app, Heremi will locate the user's address using mapping tools and act as location guide.
  4. Dynamic pricing will be implemented for delivery fees, depending on the distance.
  5. Earnings gathered are withdrawable based on the choosen payment option.
  6. Heremi takes 6% of the total earnings.
  7. An admin console, accessible through web browser, will be provided where you can manage your appointments, transactions, earnings, and more.
